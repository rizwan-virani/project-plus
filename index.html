Train for Project+.
Walk in exam-ready.

A comprehensive, self-paced, open-source training ground for the CompTIA Project+ PK0-005 certification. In-depth coverage of all four exam domains, 400 Rapid Recall flashcards on a spaced-repetition scheduler, a 400-question bank, 30 performance-based simulations plus a drag-and-drop Technical Taxonomy Mapping engine, 20 hands-on sandbox labs, a full-length timed mock exam, and an Industry Certification Explorer.

Authored & designed by Professor Rizwan Virani · San Jacinto College

How to use this platform

  1. Read the Exam Mechanics & Career Guidance cards first. Understand the landscape: scoring, timing, question styles, and where Project+ sits in a project-management career.
  2. Deep-dive the four Domain Study cards. Each opens a rigorous, scenario-driven reading interface that mirrors a full prep manual.
  3. Lock in the terms with Rapid Recall Flashcards. 100 cards per domain plus a Master Acronym Drill, powered by spaced repetition — grade each card honestly and the scheduler resurfaces what you're forgetting and retires what you know.
  4. Validate your knowledge with the Domain Quizzes. Ten focused questions per domain, drawn at random and graded with full rationale on every option.
  5. Train practical skills in the PBQ Simulators. 30 interactive performance-based questions across critical-path scheduling, risk registers, earned value, change and issue control, and procurement, plus a drag-and-drop technical taxonomy mapping engine that has you sort real-world scenarios into the correct categories.
  6. Benchmark readiness with the full-length, timed Mock Exam. Weighted exactly to the official domain percentages, with a countdown timer and a scoring dashboard.
  7. Get hands-on in the Labs. Twenty interactive sandbox labs spanning every exam domain — flip each card to review its objectives, then launch a full-screen console with live commands, configuration panels, and a project log.
  8. Plan your path in the Supplemental Learning Hub. Open the Industry Certification Explorer to compare 36 industry certifications across 12 vendors, with costs, exam blueprints, and salary and role insights.

Notice: This platform is optimized for desktop or laptop computers running the Google Chrome browser. Interactive elements, including the hands-on labs, terminal simulations, and drag-and-drop activities, require a mouse and keyboard to function correctly. These features may not display or operate properly on mobile devices.
